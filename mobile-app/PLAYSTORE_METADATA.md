## After Submission

Google Play typically reviews within 24–48 hours.
- Check Play Console for approval/rejection messages
- Common rejections: policy violations, malware flags, privacy issues
- SLP Mobile (webview wrapper) may be flagged if no native functionality exists — add native features (notifications, offline mode, etc.) to improve acceptance
